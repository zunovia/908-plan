Voxna is an app that deepens self-understanding through 30 seconds of daily voice recording.

You have a voice you cannot hear yourself.
When you hear it, the repetitions become quiet.

FEATURES
- 30-second daily voice recording
- AI-powered voice analysis (energy, clarity, expression range, tempo)
- Weekly & monthly reports to visualize changes
- Inquiry prompts based on your voice patterns

PRIVACY FIRST
- Voice recordings are never sent to any server
- All data is stored only on your device
- Only extracted metrics are used for analysis

FOR YOU IF
- You want to notice changes in your voice
- You want to reflect on daily condition through your voice
- You want to engage with your voice as part of mindfulness practice

ON YOUR WAY TO ZERO — toward the stillness within your voice.
